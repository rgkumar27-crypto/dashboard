Case competition concept

From spreadsheet rows to a dashboard people can actually use.

This version treats the project like a real data platform. Instead of asking donors and stakeholders to read spreadsheets, it organizes schools, donation sites, cities, and state totals into searchable records, summary cards, and easy-to-read tables.

Open the data View Compudopt

What this solves

  • Makes impact data more accessible and digestible.
  • Shows donation locations alongside school outcomes.
  • Turns static reporting into an interactive platform.
  • Helps donors understand significance at a glance.
Recommended framing: "Compudopt already has the work. This dashboard makes that work easier to see, track, and support."
